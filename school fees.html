How To Pay Fees Via School Pay

All fees must be paid through the designated school bank accounts, or, through the student’s code via school pay. At least 70% of the total fees should be paid for the start of the term for those who cannot pay all the 100%.

  • MTN: Dial *165*4*3*2*1#

    • Airtel: Dial *185*6*2*1#

    Select Option 1 - Pay School Fees

    Enter the Student Code / Number eg. 100*****34

    Enter the School Fees Amount to Pay to the School

    Confirm the Details of Payment Displayed to you.(School name, Student name, Class, School Fees amount, Charges)

    Continue to authorize transaction

    Enter your Mobile Money PIN

    Approve Payment

    You will Receive an SMS to confirm your payment
